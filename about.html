About Kenshō

Kenshō (見性) is an AI-powered research assistant designed to help you understand complex topics without breaking your focus.

Core Features

Explain
Get a simple, AI-powered explanation of any complex topic.
Summarize
Condense long articles or dense paragraphs into concise bullet points.
Define
Receive a quick, official dictionary-style definition for any term.
Thesaurus
Find synonyms to improve your writing and expand your vocabulary.
