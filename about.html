About Kenshō

Kenshō (見性) is an AI-powered assistant designed to help you understand, create, and refine text without breaking your focus.

Core Features

Ask Kenshō
Your direct line to a powerful AI. Ask any question, get an answer.
Explain
Get a simple, clear explanation of any complex topic.
Summarize
Condense long articles or dense paragraphs into concise bullet points.
Thesaurus
Find synonyms to improve your writing and expand your vocabulary.

Pro Features

Extract Recipe
Find a recipe online? Extract the ingredients and steps into a clean, easy-to-read format, leaving the blog story behind.
Make Professional
Rewrite text to have a more formal and professional tone.
Proofread
Correct grammar and spelling mistakes in any block of text.
Translate
Translate text between a dozen of the world's most common languages.
Define
Receive a quick, official dictionary-style definition for any term.
